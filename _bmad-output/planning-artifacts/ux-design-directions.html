Understanding your needs
Input & Output
What format are the bank statements you receive? (PDF, scanned images, CSV, other?)
What should the final output look like? A risk score? A full report? Both?
PDFs mostly, some scanned. I need a full report with a risk score between 0-1, reasoning for the score, and a recommend/deny/review decision.
Process & Rules
What specific factors do you evaluate? (income stability, debt-to-income, employment, etc.)
Are there hard rules that automatically deny an application? (e.g., DTI above 50%)
We look at DTI ratio (deny if over 43%), income stability over 2+ years, employment length, and flag any single deposit over $10k that isn't payroll. Credit score below 580 is auto-deny.
Requirements Summary
Here's what I understood — does this look right?
Input: Bank statement PDFs (some scanned)
Output: Risk report with score (0-1), reasoning, and recommend/deny/review
Rules: DTI >43% = deny, credit <580 = deny, flag deposits >$10k non-payroll
Evaluation: Income stability (2yr), employment length, DTI ratio, suspicious patterns
